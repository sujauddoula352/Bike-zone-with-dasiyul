Kawasaki Z900RS
with Smile

Lifestyle ohne Fake. Die perfekte Modern Classic Kombination aus Oldschool-Look mit den neuesten Errungenschaften und Innovationen der Technik. Die Z900RS SE ist die Antwort auf all diese Anforderungen der heutigen Straßen. Dieses Bike hat einen Öhlins-Dämpfer erhalten, sowie eine überarbeitete Gabel. Der Spirit ist klar an die Legende der 1970er angelegt, die Kawasaki Z1. Die Z900RS SE ist nicht nur eine Homage an jenes Bike, sondern technisch einfach up-to-date.

Ducati Monster 620
from Prague

This is a custom-made construction for a customer,” says Matěj. “The instructions were clear-to build a very minimalist machine based on a Ducati monster with as little comfort as possible. We have selected the most interesting parts from several Ducati and combined them in our idea of the ultimate fun machine.” With such a letter, we knew that this monster would look wild even before the photos were uploaded. And we were not disappointed. Gas & Oil started with an original 2005 Ducati Monster 620, but when it was finished, all that was left was the frame, the engine and the front forks of the donor wheel.

Daytona 675 Colors for 2010
with Smile

It was interesting looking at posts from 2007-2008 where people were asking for a white Daytona like the previous gen. Then to much surprise, Triumph releases a White special edition in 2009 as a 2010 model. So I'd like to think that Triumph trolls this forum a bit (I know ... wishful thinking). I don't have a 675 yet (saving cash for a 2nd gen). I am quite confident that if Triumph does not change anything on the bike in 2010, they will at least add 1 or 2 more color options for 2010.
